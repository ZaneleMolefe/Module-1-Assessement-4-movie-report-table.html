CAPE TOWN

Is a port city on South Africa's southwest coast, on a peninsula beneath the imposing Table mountain. Slowly rotating cable cars climb to the mountain's flat top, from which there are sweeping views of the city, the busy harbour and boats heading for Robben Island, the notorious prison that once held Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela which is now a living museum.
